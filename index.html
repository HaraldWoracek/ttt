Colloquium on Operator Theory
Vienna, 4 - 6 March 2004

on the occasion of the retirement of Heinz Langer


On the occasion of the retirement of O.Univ.Prof. Dr. Heinz Langer a Colloquium on Operator Theory will take place at the Institute for Analysis and Scientific Computing of the Vienna University of Technology.

The colloquium will start on Thursday, 4 March, 9.15 and end on Saturday, 6 March, evening.
There will be some survey lectures on topics that are related to Heinz Langer's work. The other talks will be scheduled for 30 minutes.
On Friday evening all participants and accompanying persons are invited to a conference dinner.
